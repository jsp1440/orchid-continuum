Research Overview

Our research focuses on optimizing plant-soil interactions through beneficial mycorrhizal partnerships. These naturally occurring fungal networks enhance nutrient uptake, improve soil structure, and increase plant resilience.

Key Benefits
  • Increased Nutrient Efficiency: 40-60% improvement in nutrient uptake
  • Enhanced Soil Health: Improved soil structure and water retention
  • Reduced Input Costs: Lower fertilizer and water requirements
  • Sustainable Agriculture: Natural, biological soil enhancement
Research Applications

Applications include sustainable agriculture, ecosystem restoration, and horticultural optimization. Our platform provides the tools and data needed to understand and optimize these beneficial relationships.
