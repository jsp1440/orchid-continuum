How the Orchid Continuum Works

Your complete guide to using AI-powered orchid identification and analysis

Simple 3-Step Process

1

Upload Your Orchid Photo

Take a clear photo of your orchid flower, leaf, or entire plant. Our system accepts various angles and lighting conditions for best results.

Tips: Clear focus, good lighting, close-up of flower preferred
2

AI Analysis & Identification

Our advanced AI system analyzes your photo using computer vision and cross-references with multiple botanical databases for accurate identification.

Sources: RHS, AOS, WorldPlants.de, Ecuagenera, Andy's Orchids
3

Get Comprehensive Results

Receive detailed identification results, care instructions, judging scores, parentage information, and contribution to the global database.

Results: ID, care guide, judging analysis, botanical data
