What is The Orchid Continuum?

The Orchid Continuum is a comprehensive digital research platform designed to consolidate orchid data, enable AI-powered analysis, and inspire the next generation of botanical researchers.

Created to solve:

Fragmented orchid data across multiple sources

Built with:

AI integration, GBIF APIs, and Google Drive systems

Purpose:

Enable student research and conservation efforts

What We've Already Achieved

  • 5,963 orchid records with taxonomic data
  • AI-powered image analysis using OpenAI Vision
  • GBIF integration for authoritative taxonomy
  • Interactive mapping with global distribution
  • Google Drive integration for secure photo storage
  • Advanced search & filtering capabilities
  • Real breeding analysis (F226 Sarcochilus proof)
  • Educational widgets for research learning
