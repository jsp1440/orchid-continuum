Orchid Memory Challenge

How to Play
  1. Choose your difficulty level:
    • Easy: Match identical orchid photographs
    • Medium: Match orchid photos with their genus names
    • Hard: Match photos with full scientific names
  2. Click cards to flip them over and reveal what's underneath
  3. Find matching pairs by remembering card positions
  4. Clear the entire board before time runs out to win
  5. Earn bonus points for speed and efficiency
Pro Tips
  • Start by clicking cards around the edges - they're easier to remember
  • Create a mental pattern or story to remember card locations
  • Focus on one section at a time rather than random clicking
  • Use the timer strategically - sometimes it's better to be accurate than fast
  • Learn orchid genera and species names to excel at higher difficulties

Daily Rebus Puzzles

What are Rebus Puzzles?

Rebus puzzles use pictures, symbols, and wordplay to represent orchid names or concepts. Each puzzle tells a story that leads to a specific orchid genus or species name.

How to Solve
  1. Look at the visual clues - each emoji or symbol represents part of the answer
  2. Think about orchid terminology - the answer is always orchid-related
  3. Consider wordplay - sometimes it's about how words sound, not just their meaning
  4. Use the hint button if you're stuck - it provides additional context
  5. Submit your answer - genus names, common names, or scientific names are all accepted

Scoring System

Base Points
Easy: 100 pts/pair
Medium: 200 pts/pair
Hard: 300 pts/pair
Rebus: 50 pts
Time Bonuses
  • Speed bonus: 2 points per second remaining
  • Quick completion: Extra bonus for finishing under 50% of time limit
  • Lightning round: Special bonus for sub-30-second completions
Efficiency Bonuses
  • Perfect game: Match every pair in minimum moves
  • Near perfect: Within 150% of minimum moves
  • Efficiency multiplier: Fewer mistakes = higher bonuses

Badges & Achievement System

How Badges Work

Badges are earned automatically when you achieve certain milestones. They're displayed on your profile and show your progress as an orchid gaming enthusiast.

Badge Categories
  • Beginner Badges: First-time achievements
  • Skill Badges: Demonstrate mastery of different game types
  • Speed Badges: Fast completion times
  • Accuracy Badges: Perfect or near-perfect games
  • Dedication Badges: Playing multiple games or consecutive days
  • Knowledge Badges: Excel at harder difficulty levels
