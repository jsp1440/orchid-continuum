How to Use This Globe

Welcome to the Orchid Climate Globe!
Explore how Earth's light, climate, and habitats shape orchid diversity. Here's how to get started:

🔹 Basic Controls

  • Spin the globe → Click and drag.
  • Zoom in/out → Pinch (touch) or scroll (mouse).
  • Tap hotspots → Colored dots show rainforest, desert, mountain, climate, or orchid locations.

🔹 Legend Guide

  • 🟢 Rainforests
  • 🟤 Mountains
  • 🟠 Deserts
  • 🟣 Climate Zones
  • 🌸 Orchid Hotspots

🔹 Tools & Buttons

  • Random Feature 🎲 → Jump to a surprise hotspot.
  • Demo Tour 🎥 → Watch an automatic walkthrough.
  • Day/Night Toggle 🌞🌙 → See Earth's sunlight boundary.
  • 35th Parallel Trail 🌐 → Follow orchids across continents.
  • Speed Slider ⏩ → Adjust globe rotation speed.

🔹 Tips

  • Some hotspots reveal orchid species cards with photos, pollinators, and fun facts.
  • Use the legend chips to turn categories on/off.
  • Look for the mini-panel showing sunspots, UV, moon phase, and photoperiod.
