Featured Orchid Vendors

Seven of California's talented orchid growers and hybridizers participated:

Ambriz Kingdom of Plants

Laelia, Cattleya, Epidendrum orchids suitable for outdoor growing plus amazing Tillandsias (air plants)

OrchidDesign

Outdoor Cymbidiums, exotic Vandas, and Dendrobium orchids

Orchids of Los Osos

Unique species including pansy orchids (Miltoniopsis)

Sorella Orchids

Unique Cymbidium hybrids and specialty orchids

CalCoast Orchids

Phalaenopsis from giants to "teacup" varieties, plus Cymbidium seedlings

Tim Culbertson

Paphiopedilum slipper orchids for windowsill growing

Ecuagenera

Cool and warm growing orchids from Ecuador plus exotic foliage

Additional Vendors

Beauti Florist (air plants, Hoyas), Fang Hua Garden (Asian Cymbidiums)
