About Five Cities Orchid Society

Five Cities Orchid Society is located on the Central California Coast, mid-way between Los Angeles and San Francisco.

We enjoy a temperate Mediterranean climate allowing us to grow many types of orchids outdoors. We serve not only our local orchid community of FCOS members but through our monthly Zoom meetings and monthly newsletter we now reach a global audience.

Our Mission: We help our members grow better orchids through education, community support, and shared expertise.

Garden Talks Program

FCOS members present educational talks to local garden clubs, plant nurseries and other social organizations throughout the Central California Coast.

Available Presentations:
  • Part 1: Growing Phalaenopsis (moth) orchids and getting them to re-bloom
  • Part 2: Wide range of orchids for outdoor growing in frost-free coastal locations
  • Part 3: Advanced techniques for the Central California Coast

Our Unique Growing Climate

Winter Conditions

Low 40°F minimum
Rarely frost
Cool nights ideal

Summer Conditions

Rarely above 90°F
Cool nights 60-68°F
Ocean influence

Outdoor Growing

Many species thrive
Frost-free areas

Orchids That Thrive Outdoors Here:
  • Cymbidium species & hybrids
  • Cattleya alliance
  • Laelia species
  • Dendrobium (many types)
  • Epidendrum species
  • Vanda alliance
  • Even equatorial species from 3600+ ft elevation
  • Cool-growing Pleurothallids
