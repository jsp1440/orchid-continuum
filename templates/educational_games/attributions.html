Citation Guidelines

When using data or images from this platform, please cite:
APA Format:
Five Cities Orchid Society. (2025). The Orchid Continuum: AI-Enhanced Digital Orchid Database. Retrieved [Date], from [URL]. Images courtesy of Gary Yong Gee Orchid Collection.
For Gary Yong Gee Photo Collection:
Yong Gee, G. (2025). Gary Yong Gee Orchid Database. Retrieved [Date], from https://orchids.yonggee.name
For World Orchids Taxonomic Data:
Hassler, M. (2025). World Orchids: World Plants complete list of orchid families, genera, and species. Retrieved [Date], from http://worldplants.webarchiv.kit.edu/
