Sarcochilus

Barrita Sarcochilus hybrids are renowned for producing some of the finest examples of these exceptional Australian orchids. Our breeding program prioritises crucial factors such as ease of growth, prolific spike production, and a vibrant range of colours, including the latest hues. Our dedicated focus on these key traits ensures that our hybrids not only thrive effortlessly but also offer stunning, diverse displays of colour. This selection provides you with access to our most recent and advanced hybrids, reflecting the forefront of our breeding efforts. Explore the latest innovations and experience the exceptional quality of our Sarcochilus hybrids firsthand.
