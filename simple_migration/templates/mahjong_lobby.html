How to Play

Match pairs of identical orchid tiles to clear the board. Only free tiles (not blocked by others) can be selected.

Orchid Suits: Cattleya (purple), Dendrobium (blue), Phalaenopsis (pink)
Honor Tiles: AOS Awards (AM, FCC, HCC, CBR)
Dragon Tiles: Growing conditions (Temperature, Light, Water)

Basic Match: 10 points
Speed Bonus: Extra points for fast matches
Clear Bonus: 500 points for clearing the board
Multiplayer: Compete for highest score!
