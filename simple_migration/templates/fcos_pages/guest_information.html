Welcome Guests!

Your guide to visiting Five Cities Orchid Society

We Help Our Members Grow Better Orchids

Five Cities Orchid Society is located on the Central California Coast, mid-way between Los Angeles and San Francisco. We enjoy a temperate Mediterranean climate allowing us to grow many types of orchids outdoors.

We serve not only our local orchid community but through our monthly Zoom meetings and newsletter we now reach a global audience. You're invited to join us!
